Stories

Here I share reflections that run along the common thread of several films. This rubric is free and with no boundaries, embracing movies from all eras and countries. Each film has something unique to offer, a common thread that ties them together in the eyes of the beholder. The goal is to share with those who read what was given to me by watching the films I will write about. These are not reviews, nor criticism, or perhaps yes, to the extent that these terms are capable of conveying what has been seen more than what has been shown.
